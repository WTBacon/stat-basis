The Answer of Practice 1-2

2019-03-19 · 109 words · 1 minute read
probability
chapter-1

問2

$$ 事象 A_1, A_2, A_3 に対して次の等式を示せ.\\
P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B) \quad より \\
\begin{align} P(A_1 \cup (A_2 \cup A_3)) &= P(A_1) + P(A_2 \cup A_3) - P(A_1 \cap (A_2 \cup A_3)) \\
&= P(A_1) + P(A_2) + P(A_3) - P(A_2 \cap A_3) - P(A_1 \cap (A_2 \cup A_3)) \\
&= P(A_1) + P(A_2) + P(A_3) - P(A_2 \cap A_3) - (P(A_1 \cap A_3) + P(A_1 \cap A_2) - P(A_1 \cap A_2 \cap A_3)) \\
&= P(A_1) + P(A_2) + P(A_3) - P(A_1 \cap A_2) - P(A_2 \cap A_3) - P(A_3 \cap A_1) + P(A_1 \cap A_2 \cap A_3) \Box \end{align} $$
